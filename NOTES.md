### Level construction

- Made up of a series of "encounters"
- Encounter is a series of spawns
- Encounter can be empty
- Encounters can be tagged/themed
- Level can randomly generate a series of themed encounters
- Encounter can be a boss encounter. These can be tagged special so they dont show up randomly
- Need a database of encounters.
